My UX Skills

User Research

I approach user research as a means for understanding the user's needs, behaviors, experience and motivations. Through various qualitative and quantitative methods, I am able to better inform the process of solving for user's problems.

User Empathy

In order to have a successful design project, it is essential to understand and empathize with the user's. Throughout the entire design process, I gather real insights about the user so that there is no guess work involved.

Qualitative Interviews

I conduct qualitative interviews to pull insight out of the user's. My approach involves setting primary and secondary research goals, defining a set of open questions, and constantly asking why throughout the interview process.
